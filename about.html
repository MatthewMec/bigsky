Toys and a Dream

Big Sky Bricks was imagined to life from a childhood dream. A small town store with big building ideas. We want to inspire fun in kids and adults alike. Come buy, trade, and build new and used Lego sets in our store on Main Street.

We carry the latest Lego sets and sets from other brick makers. We also carry retired and used sets that are hard or impossible to find. We love to discuss anything Lego and look forward to seeing you in the store!

Looking for an idea for a birthday party? Look no further than Big Sky Bricks. We have a variety of party options for all budgets and have a fun and unique party plan for you. Our after school programs are also a great way for kids to spend their afternoons having fun while learning. Check those out! You won't want to miss them.

We look forward to seeing you in the store and visiting with us about all things Lego. A fun environment that brings the joy for kids of all ages. We'll see you soon!
