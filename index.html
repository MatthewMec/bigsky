Big Sky Bricks Site Plan

Purpose

Big Sky Bricks is a locally owned Lego store in Kalispell, Montana, offering a wide selection of Lego sets, minifigures, and accessories. Our website will showcase our products, highlight special events, and provide store information to customers.

Audience

Our target audience includes Lego enthusiasts of all ages, collectors, and parents looking for quality toys for their children. We also cater to tourists visiting Kalispell who are interested in unique Lego finds.
