Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer birthday party packages?

Yes! We have a variety of party options for all budgets. Each package includes building activities, games, and a dedicated party host. Contact us for more details and availability.

What age groups are your after school programs for?

Our after school programs are designed for children ages 5-12. We group children by age to ensure appropriate challenges and activities.

Do you buy used Lego sets?

Absolutely! We buy and trade used Lego sets. Bring your sets to the store for an evaluation, or contact us with photos and details for a preliminary quote.

Can I special order a set if I don't see it in the store?

Yes, we can special order most current Lego sets. For retired sets, we'll do our best to locate them through our network of collectors and partners.

Do you offer discounts for schools or educational programs?

We offer special pricing for educational institutions and school programs. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and requirements.
